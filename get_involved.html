Curated Commons

Curated Commons is by the people for the people. As a member of the commons or as a part of our collection, we would like to hear your voice.

Get curated

Chance and whim decides who we curate, photograph, label, and enter into our collection. If you were curated recently, please go here. We want to give art a voice. If you were not yet curated - but you would like to - check our movements on twitter. Chance may need a helping hand.

Get in touch

Tweet about Curated Commons. Let us and everyone know your thoughts. Join our twitter feed to learn where and when we calatogue. Visit our Facebook page. If you are not on twitter, check us out on Facebook to learn where we curate. Leave a comment, on the project or the art. Email us at curated.commons.at.org. We would love to hear from you.

Be kind to the art

Art is fragile and in need of protection. Harsh words may cause harm and are not the purpose of this project.
