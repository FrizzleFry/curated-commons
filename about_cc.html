Curated Commons

Inspired by the scenes and scenery near our homes in Brooklyn and Manhattan, we founded Curatted Commons in order to capture and present the art of the instant, created by the circumstances of the commons, continuously blinking in and out of existence. We have had no hand in creating the artifacts collected in this gallery--we are merely curators and collectors.

As art historians of the infintessimaly narrow frontier between present and past, we choose to remain in the back room of the gallery, letting the pieces speak for themselves in the front.

Pieces displayed in the Gallery are catalogued as they are collected.

The artifacts evident in the pieces we exhibit are issued a physical catalogue card at the time that we enter the piece into the Gallery: immediately after the piece is created and then annihilated.

Enjoy.

Our collection methods are irregular and dictated by chance and whim, just as are the procceses guiding the creation of the pieces themsleves.
